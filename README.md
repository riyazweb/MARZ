# MARSZ — Last Hope

**A 3D Mars exploration game where you talk to AI aliens using your voice to save Earth.**

> The year is 2164 AD. Earth is dying. You are Ryan, an astronaut who crash-landed on Mars. Two alien survivors live here — Zorg and Xylia. Talk to them, earn their trust, and send a signal back to Earth before it's too late.

## Why This Game Is Different

This is not a regular game with pre-written dialogues. Every conversation with the aliens is **generated in real-time by AI**. The aliens listen to what you say (using your microphone), think about it, and respond with unique dialogue and voice every single time.

This is what the **future of gaming** looks like:
- **AI-powered NPCs** that actually understand what you say
- **Voice input** — you speak into your mic, the alien hears you
- **Voice output** — the alien talks back with a real AI-generated voice
- **No two playthroughs are the same** — every conversation is unique
- **Trust system** — the aliens remember how you treated them and react accordingly

## Features
- **🧠 AI-Powered NPC Dialogue — Aliens respond using Mistral AI (mistral-large-latest). Every response is unique. No two conversations are ever the same.**
- **🎙️ Voice Input — Web Speech API converts your speech to text in real-time. Speak naturally — the alien hears every word.**
- **🔊 Voice Output — ElevenLabs Text-to-Speech gives each alien a unique AI-generated voice. They actually talk back.**
- **⚡ Trust / Reputation System — A 0–100 score that changes based on how you talk to the aliens. Be respectful to gain trust, be rude and lose it.**
- **🕸️ Hive Mind System — Both aliens share a live reputation score. What you do with one alien affects how the other treats you. They are mentally linked.**
- **📢 Gossip System — After every conversation, an AI-generated gossip summary is shared between aliens. If you're rude to Zorg, Xylia will already know.**
- **🧬 Memory Link — Each alien maintains a full conversation history (up to 16 messages). They remember everything you said earlier in the session.**
- **💭 Thought Bubbles — See the alien's inner monologue generated by AI. What they think vs what they say out loud.**
- **🌍 3D Open World — Explore a Mars landscape with sand terrain, atmospheric fog, cliffs, NASA rovers, a UFO, and your crashed ship.**
- **📡 Scanner (C key) — Scan nearby objects to get intel. Shows name, distance, and interaction options.**
- **⏳ Extraction Mechanic — Hold E near the Perseverance rover. A circular progress ring fills over 3 seconds to extract signal data.**
- **📻 Earth-Comm Terminal — Open the UFO terminal, enter coordinates, and transmit the final signal to Earth.**
- **🎬 Cinematic Intro — Voiced narration plays with timed full-screen story text slides. Skippable.**
- **🖥️ Fullscreen Mode — Game enters fullscreen automatically on START.**
- **🎮 3 Difficulty Modes — Easy (+25 trust bonus), Medium (+15), Hard (raw AI scoring only). Completely different experience each mode.**
- **🏁 Mission Complete Ending — White beam flash, aliens fade to dust, radio crackle from Earth, ending screen.**
- **🛸 HUD Overlay — Live AI telemetry showing neural link status, NPC emotion, reputation score, and scanner data.**



## Game Story

The year is **2164 AD**. 150 years of pollution have destroyed Earth. The oceans are black. The air is iron. Humanity is dying.

You are **Ryan**, an astronaut sent on a one-way mission to Mars. During entry, your ship loses signal with Earth. Systems fail. You crash into the red dust.

You are alone... or so you think.

Two alien survivors live on Mars — **Zorg**, a scientist, and **Xylia**, his wife. Their civilization died the same way Earth is dying now: pollution consumed everything until the atmosphere collapsed.

They have a **UFO with a transmitter** that can send a signal to Earth. But they don't trust humans. You remind them of the destroyers who killed their world.

**Your mission:** Talk to the aliens. Earn their trust. Get access to the UFO. Find the Perseverance rover. Extract the signal data. Transmit it to Earth. Save humanity.

## How to Play — Full Guide

### Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **W A S D** | Move your character |
| **Shift** | Run |
| **Hold ALT** | Talk to nearby alien (voice input) |
| **C** | Scan nearby objects |
| **Hold E** | Extract data / Use terminals |
| **Escape** | Close terminal |
| **Mic Button** | Hold to talk (on-screen button) |

### Game Flow (Step by Step)

1. **Start Screen** — Click START (game goes fullscreen)
2. **Controls Screen** — Shows you the controls, click CONTINUE
3. **Difficulty Select** — Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard
4. **Cinematic Intro** — Voice narration plays with story text (you can skip)
5. **Game Starts** — You spawn on Mars with your crashed ship nearby

### How to Win

1. **Find the aliens** — Zorg and Xylia are on opposite sides of the map. Walk around.
2. **Talk to them** — Hold ALT or press the mic button. Speak naturally. Be respectful.
3. **Build trust** — The trust bar at the bottom shows your reputation (0-100). Be sincere about Earth dying. Connect with their story.
4. **Unlock the UFO** — Once trust reaches 70+, the aliens let you use their UFO transmitter.
5. **Find Perseverance rover** — Walk to the NASA Perseverance rover. Press C to scan it.
6. **Extract data** — Hold E near Perseverance to extract signal coordinates (takes 3 seconds).
7. **Go to UFO** — Walk to the alien UFO. Press E to open the Earth-Comm terminal.
8. **Send signal** — The coordinates auto-fill. Click TRANSMIT.
9. **Mission Complete** — Earth receives the signal. You saved humanity.

### Difficulty Levels

| Difficulty | Trust Bonus | How It Feels |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Easy** | +25 per good interaction | Aliens warm up fast. 5 min game. |
| **Medium** | +15 per good interaction | Need 3-4 good conversations. 10 min game. |
| **Hard** | No bonus (raw AI scoring) | Aliens are hostile. Every word matters. |

## How the Game Logic Works

### Frontend (index.ts)

1. **Scene Setup** — Creates Three.js scene with Mars-colored sky (orange), fog, sand floor, directional lighting with shadows
2. **Screen Flow** — Title → Controls → Difficulty → Cinematic Intro (with voice) → Game
3. **Player Model** — Loads `mercenary_astronaut.glb` with Mixamo animations (idle, walk, run)
4. **NPC Spawn** — Two aliens spawned at fixed positions on opposite sides of the map
5. **Voice Input** — When you hold ALT, Web Speech API captures your speech, sends transcript to backend
6. **Chat Display** — Shows NPC name, dialogue text, and thought bubble on screen
7. **Trust Bar** — Visual bar that fills from red → orange → gold as reputation increases
8. **Scanner** — C key checks distance to nearest scannable object, shows info
9. **Extraction** — Hold E near Perseverance, circular progress ring fills over 3 seconds
10. **Terminal** — E near UFO opens Earth-Comm input, TRANSMIT button sends signal
11. **Ending** — White beam, aliens fade to dust, radio message from Earth, mission complete screen

### Backend (server.js)

1. **NPC Personas** — Each alien has a detailed system prompt with story, behavior rules, and difficulty adjustments
2. **Mistral AI Chat** — Sends conversation history + system prompt to Mistral, gets JSON response with dialogue, emotion, thought, reputation change
3. **Reputation System** — Global score 0-100, starts at 50. Each conversation changes it. At 70+, UFO unlocks.
4. **Difficulty Scaling** — Easy gives flat +25 bonus per positive interaction, Medium +15, Hard uses raw AI scoring only
5. **Gossip System** — After each conversation, a summary is generated and shared between aliens
6. **ElevenLabs TTS** — Converts alien dialogue to speech audio, sent as base64 to frontend
7. **Game State** — Tracks reputation, core unlock, data extraction, gossip across all API calls
8. **JSON Response Format** — AI always responds with structured JSON: thought, dialogue, emotion, action, reputation_change

### NPC Behavior (npc.ts)

- Each NPC stores its **spawn position** and wanders within an **8-unit radius**
- Movement speed is **1.2 units/sec** (slow wandering)
- When player gets within **4 units**, NPC stops and faces the player
- First approach triggers an **auto-greet** (NPC speaks first)
- NPCs avoid getting too close to each other (4-unit avoid radius)

## Setup & Run

### 1. Install

```bash
npm install
```

### 2. Create `.env` file

```env
MISTRAL_API_KEY=your_mistral_api_key
ELEVENLABS_API_KEY=your_elevenlabs_api_key
ELEVENLABS_VOICE_ALIEN1=JBFqnCBsd6RMkjVDRZzb
ELEVENLABS_VOICE_ALIEN2=EXAVITQu4vr4xnSDxMaL
```

- Get Mistral API key from [console.mistral.ai](https://console.mistral.ai)
- Get ElevenLabs API key from [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io)
- Voice IDs are optional (defaults are provided)

### 3. Start the backend

```bash
node server.js
```

Server runs on `http://localhost:3001`

### 4. Build & serve the frontend

```bash
 npm run serve 
```

Then open `src/index.html` in your browser (or use a local server).

## API Routes

| Route | Method | What It Does |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| `/api/npc-chat` | POST | Send player message to an alien, get AI response + audio |
| `/api/set-difficulty` | POST | Set game difficulty (easy/medium/hard), resets game state |
| `/api/extract` | POST | Extract data from Perseverance rover |
| `/api/send-signal` | POST | Transmit final signal to Earth |
| `/api/game-state` | GET | Get current reputation, unlock status, extraction status |
